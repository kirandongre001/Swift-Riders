User stories for a Swift Rider from the perspective of a driver:

1. As a driver, I want to create a profile with my vehicle details, so that potential passengers can see information about my car before requesting a ride.

2. As a driver, I want to set my availability and schedule in the app, so that I can plan my carpooling trips in advance.

3. As a driver, I want to receive ride requests with details about the potential passengers, their pick-up and drop-off locations, and the estimated fare, so that I can make informed decisions about accepting rides.

4. As a driver, I want to have the option to review the ratings and feedback of potential passengers before accepting a ride, so that I can ensure a positive experience.

5. As a driver, I want to be able to set preferences for the gender or number of passengers I am willing to accept, so that I can feel more comfortable and in control of my carpooling experience.

6. As a driver, I want the app to provide real-time navigation and traffic information to optimize the route and ETA, so that I can efficiently pick up and drop off passengers.

7. As a driver, I want to have the flexibility to communicate with passengers through the app's messaging system, so that I can coordinate pick-up details and inform them about any delays.

8. As a driver, I want the app to automatically calculate and handle the fare based on factors like distance, time, and the number of passengers, so that the payment process is transparent and convenient.

9. As a driver, I want to have the option to split the fare among passengers, so that the cost is shared fairly.

10. As a driver, I want to receive notifications about potential passengers who are nearby and looking for a ride, so that I can proactively offer carpooling services.

11. As a driver, I want the app to provide a secure and reliable payment system, so that I can receive payment from passengers without concerns about fraud or payment issues.

12. As a driver, I want to be able to rate and provide feedback about passengers after each trip, so that I can contribute to building a trustworthy carpooling community.

13. As a driver, I want the app to provide information about the destination and any specific instructions from passengers, so that I can plan the drop-off accordingly.

14. As a driver, I want to have access to customer support through the app in case of any issues or emergencies during a carpooling trip.

15. As a driver, I want the app to keep track of my earnings and provide detailed summaries for accounting purposes, so that I can easily manage my finances related to carpooling.

These user stories cover various aspects of the Swift Rider experience for a driver, ensuring a seamless and user-friendly interaction with the carpooling system.
